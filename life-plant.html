Aloe Vera growing at home – simple, tough, and very useful.

Aloe Vera is one of the most useful and low-maintenance plants you can grow at home. It’s not just a pretty succulent — it’s a natural first aid kit, a beauty treatment, and an important part of traditional Jamaican remedies.

Benefits of Aloe Vera

Skin Care

Aloe Vera gel can help soothe minor burns, sunburns, cuts, insect bites and skin irritation.

Hair & Scalp Health

Aloe is also good for the scalp and works well in DIY hair masks.

Traditional Uses in Jamaica

  • Cold and flu mixes with honey and lime.
  • Hair treatments with castor oil.
  • Skin healing for minor cuts.

How I Grow Aloe Vera at Home

  • Light: Bright light, not harsh sun.
  • Water: Let soil dry between watering.
  • Soil: Well-draining soil.
  • Containers: Pots or recycled containers.

About Gardening With Kev

Gardening With Kev is a simple home gardening blog based in Jamaica. It focuses on practical, natural ways to grow plants using everyday materials and real-life experience.

The goal is to show that you don’t need expensive tools or perfect conditions to start growing. You can begin right where you are, using what you already have.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Simple gardening tips for beginners
  • Container gardening using bottles, buckets, and tins
  • Traditional and herbal plant uses
  • Real examples from a Jamaican home garden

About Kevin

Hi, I’m Kevin. I enjoy growing plants at home and sharing what I learn along the way. My approach is simple: start small, stay consistent, and use what you have.

Through Gardening With Kev, I hope to encourage more people to try growing their own plants, whether it’s for food, health, or just the joy of watching something grow.

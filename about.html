About Gardening With Kev

Hi, I’m Kevin

I’m a Jamaican who loves plants, and I started my home garden using whatever I had available — simple, practical, and convenient. Most of my gardening is done right where I live — in pots, buckets, recycled containers, cut-off bottles, and anything else I can find.

Gardening With Kev is my space to share what I’m learning as I grow: what works, what doesn’t, and the small wins that keep me excited about plants — and to share that journey with others.

How Gardening With Kev Started

This blog started from a simple idea: you don’t need a big yard, fancy flower pots, or expensive tools to grow healthy plants.

Over time, friends and family kept asking questions like:

  • “How you get your plants to look so healthy?”
  • “What you use on your flowers?”
  • “How you plant in those little bottles?”

Instead of answering one person at a time, I decided to document the journey here — with real photos, real experiments, and real results from my yard in Jamaica.

My Gardening Style

My approach is:

  • Simple – Use what you have first.
  • Natural – Prefer gentle, plant-friendly methods (like neem oil) where possible.
  • Practical – Focus on what works in real Jamaican conditions: heat, rain, limited space.
  • Honest – If something fails, I’ll say so. If it works, I’ll show you.

Plants I grow:

  • Aloe vera
  • Fever grass (lemongrass)
  • Life plant (Wonder of the World)
  • Pineapples

Plants I want to start growing:

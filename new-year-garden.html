As we enter the new year and we start to make new years resolutions, I want to encourage you to start your own home garden.

You don't need fancy pots; a recycled 5-gallon bottle works perfectly.

It might not be you, but the soil you are planting in. It can also be the seeds or planting materials you are using. If you start with bad soil, you can't expect a good result!

Starting small with water bottles before transplanting.

Apart from the joy you get from planting something and seeing it grow, look at the prices in the markets‚Äîespecially after the hurricane. Growing even a little bit of your own food makes a big difference for your pocket.

Let's make 2026 the year we take control of what we grow. Start small, start right, and let's grow together!

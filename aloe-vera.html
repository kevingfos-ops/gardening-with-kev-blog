Benefits of Aloe Vera

Skin Care

Aloe Vera gel can help soothe minor burns, sunburns, cuts, insect bites and skin irritation. It cools the skin and adds moisture without making it feel greasy.

Hair & Scalp Health

Aloe is also good for the scalp. It can help with dryness and flaking when used gently on the scalp, and it works well in DIY hair masks with natural oils like castor or coconut oil.

Digestion Support
